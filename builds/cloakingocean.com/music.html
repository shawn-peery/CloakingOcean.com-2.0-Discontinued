Music Introduction

Since about 5th grade, I've been able to play the piano! I've practiced on and off throughout a couple of years! With the help of my school and my grandmother, I've been able to improve my piano skills quite drastically from when I first started. However, even before learning to play the piano, music was a huge part of my life! I listen to music almost any chance I get!

Learning the Piano

My first piano experience started with my grandmother. She taught me very simple and short songs at the beginning like mary had a little lamb. I began to feel like I was cheating learning because instead of translating notes into their letters and then finding the key corresponding to that letter, I would just look at how many keys away a note was and just play it that way. I thought I would never be able to improve my skills with the piano because of that. However, my father and grandmother continued to push me and encourage me. Eventually, I found that I was actually playing the notes correctly rather than my previous method!

My grandmother taught me mainly classical music and hymns. I learned to play songs from Bach, Beethoven, Mozart, and more! It was quite exciting to learn how to perform such classics from the beginning! Attending a piano class after school, I also learned many lullabies and modern songs! Having this mix of genres made it exciting to play and learn more! Recently, I've continued to learn hymns as well as learning many popular pop songs!

Music I Enjoy

Being a religious person, I've always loved hymns and songs similar to them! I also found myself being quite content with many different genres. My mood dictates what style I desire. One of my favorite bands, when I was younger, was The Black Eyed Peas. Over the years I've highly enjoyed songs from Panic at the Disco, Secondhand Serenade, Maroon 5, Paramore, Echosmith, Coldplay and more! The genres I don't enjoy as much are rap and screamo music.

Recently I've also enjoyed many electronic songs from TheFatRat and Alan Walker! I've started enjoying the Dubstep and EDM genres more and more as I've explored their music. I also really enjoy chill genres like Cavetown and Khai Dreams. Music is a huge part of my life!
